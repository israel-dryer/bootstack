<div class="bs-features">
  <div class="bs-feature">
    <h3 class="bs-feature__title">Themed widgets</h3>
    <p class="bs-feature__desc">Six accent colors, three variants, light and dark themes — every widget styled the same way without writing a line of theme code.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="bs-feature">
    <h3 class="bs-feature__title">Bootstrap Icons built in</h3>
    <p class="bs-feature__desc">Bootstrap Icons ship with the framework — first-class in Buttons, Labels, nav, and toolbars. Sized, themed, and recolored to match.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="bs-feature">
    <h3 class="bs-feature__title">Reactive signals</h3>
    <p class="bs-feature__desc">Bind state to UI without <code>StringVar</code>s or trace callbacks — values propagate automatically.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="bs-feature">
    <h3 class="bs-feature__title">Declarative layout</h3>
    <p class="bs-feature__desc">Direction, gaps, alignment, and weights — declared on the container and broadcast to every child.</p>
<pre class="bs-feature__code"><code>PackFrame(direction="row", gap=8)
GridFrame(columns=3, gap=16)</code></pre>
  </div>
  <div class="bs-feature">
    <h3 class="bs-feature__title">Localization</h3>
    <p class="bs-feature__desc">Translate UI strings, format numbers and dates, and switch locales at runtime.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="bs-feature">
    <h3 class="bs-feature__title">Scaffold-to-ship CLI</h3>
    <p class="bs-feature__desc"><code>bootstack start</code> scaffolds. <code>bootstack build</code> packages. From zero to .exe in minutes.</p>
  </div>
</div>
